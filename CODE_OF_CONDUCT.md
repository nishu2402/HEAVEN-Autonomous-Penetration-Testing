# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

HEAVEN is a community of security researchers, pen-testers, and engineers
who build defensive and authorized-offensive tooling. We pledge to make
participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility, apologizing to those affected by mistakes,
  and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community

Behavior that will not be tolerated:

- Sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (physical / email address /
  social media handles tied to anonymous accounts) without explicit permission
- **Using the issue tracker to coordinate or solicit unauthorized
  attacks on third-party systems**
- **Sharing exploitation outputs against systems the operator does not
  have authorization to test**
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in
  a professional setting

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the
standards above. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action
in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions
that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate
reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (issue tracker,
discussions, pull requests, project social media) and also when an
individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported to the project maintainer at the contact listed in the
README "Authors" section. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.

All maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the
reporter of any incident.
